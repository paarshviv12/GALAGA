## 🌟 About The Project

Welcome to the **GALAGA Fan Project**! This project is a dedicated tribute to the legendary 1981 arcade shooter, **Galaga**. We have crafted a beautiful, interactive, and nostalgic web experience that brings the magic of the arcade right to your browser. 

The website is designed with a retro-modern aesthetic, featuring a dynamic video background, classic pixel art enemies, and even an interactive arcade machine to play the game!

### ✨ Key Features

- **🎮 Play the Classic:** Jump right into the action with an embedded, playable Galaga arcade emulator.
- **👾 Galaga Encyclopedia:** Explore detailed, scrollable catalogs of the game's iconic enemies, including:
  - **Main Enemies** (Zakos, Goeis, Boss Galagas, Captured Fighter)
  - **Transformed Enemies** (Ogawamushis, Eis, Galbosses)
  - **Special Enemies** (Tonbo, Momiji, Spaceship)
- **📰 Latest News:** Stay up to date with the newest Galaga community news, from new world records to the legendary no-fire cheat mystery.
- **🛒 Merch Shop:** Browse through our hand-picked retro merchandise like T-Shirts and Keychains.
- **🕰️ Game History:** Dive deep into the lore and legacy of Galaga.

---

## 📂 Project Structure

- **`index.html`** - The main landing page filled with news, character lists, and an interactive arcade.
- **`game.html`** - A dedicated page focused on the Galaga gameplay.
- **`history.html`** - Learn about the origins and history of the arcade hit.
- **`shop.html`** - A mock storefront for awesome retro gear like t-shirts and keychains.
- **`style.css`** - The custom stylesheets combining modern visuals with retro flair.
- **`script.js`** - Powers dynamic interactions, background unmuting, and slider logic!

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Scroll down to the **Arcade Machine** section on the home page.
2. Click **"Tap to Play"**.
3. Defeat the insect-like alien swarms and retrieve any captured fighters to double your firepower!
